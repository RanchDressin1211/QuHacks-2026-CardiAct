Predicting the Future of Heart Health One Pulse at a Time.

CardiAct is a free online model that assists doctors with diagnosing heart disease efficiently, while only using basic medical information.

CardiAct enables users to detect issues before they become severe, preventing heart attacks and saving lives.

The convenient and accessible tools offered by CardiAct speed up the diagnostic process for cardiologists and emergency personnel

Designed with transparency and responsibility in mind, our platform aims to assist—not replace—doctors, helping them focus more time on patient care while enabling earlier, more informed decision-making.

"the most crucial step toward healing is having the right diagnosis. If the disease is precisely identified, a good resolution is far more likely. Conversely, a bad diagnosis usually means a bad outcome, no matter how skilled the physician.”


- Dr. Andrew Weil, Cardiologist
