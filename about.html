Our Mission.

We are a team of high school programmers building a machine learning–based tool to support clinicians in assessing heart disease risk. Our goal is to reduce the burden on medical professionals by providing fast, data-driven insights that complement clinical judgment. Designed with transparency and responsibility in mind, our platform aims to assist—not replace—doctors, helping them focus more time on patient care while enabling earlier, more informed decision-making.
